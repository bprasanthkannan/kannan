       IDENTIFICATION DIVISION.                               
       PROGRAM-ID. MEM13.                                     
       ENVIRONMENT DIVISION.                                  
       DATA DIVISION.                                         
       WORKING-STORAGE SECTION.                               
       77 P PIC 99 VALUE 13.                                  
       77 N PIC 99.                                           
       77 I PIC 99.                                           
       77 Q PIC 99.                                           
       77 R PIC 99.                                           
       77 F PIC 99 VALUE 1.                                   
       PROCEDURE DIVISION.                                    
           COMPUTE N = P / 2.                                 
           DISPLAY N.                                         
           PERFORM PARA1 VARYING I FROM 2 BY 1 UNTIL I > N.   
           IF F EQUAL 1                                       
           DISPLAY "PRIME"                             
          ELSE                                         
           DISPLAY " NOT A PRIME"                      
          END-IF.                                      
          STOP RUN.                                    
      PARA1.                                           
          DIVIDE P BY I GIVING Q REMAINDER R.          
          DISPLAY R.                                   
          IF R EQUAL 0                                 
           ADD 1 TO F                                  
          END-IF.
